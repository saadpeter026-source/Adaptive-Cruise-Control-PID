## Individual PID Components
* **Proportional (P):**
  $$P = K_p \cdot e(t)$$

* **Integral (I):**
  $$I = K_i \int_{0}^{t} e(\tau) d\tau$$

* **Derivative (D):**
  $$D = K_d \frac{de(t)}{dt}$$
