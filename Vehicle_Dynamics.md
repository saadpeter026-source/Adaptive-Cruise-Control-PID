## Physical Representation (FBD)
u: Engine Force (Control Input)

F_drag: Aerodynamic Drag

F_g: Force due to road grade (gravity)

F_N: Normal Force

W: Vehicle Weight (m * g)

## Mathematical Equation
$$ma = u - F_{drag} - F_{g}$$
